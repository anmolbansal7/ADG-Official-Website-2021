FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

What are the domains in ADG, is it only iOS Dev?

The following are the technical domains in ADG:
IOS dev
Android Dev
Web Dev
ML
Design
Competitive Coding (compulsory)
Apart from these, we have Management, Design and Video Editing.
Choose whatever suits you the best.

How many events does ADG conduct in a year? And what kinds of events are they?

ADG usually conducts 2 events during both the technical and the cultural fests. One of these is the flagship event the "iOS Fusion" where one gets to learn specifically about Swift and its scope by getting some hands on practice during the workshop. The other event changes every year. It usually explores new domains and open doors to the world of virtual or augmented reality and so on. During the cultural fest the events conducted are a blend of innovation and tech. A few events hosted in the past years include a treasure hunt and a meme making contest. We also conduct a Hackathon, branded as Hackgrid, every year!! ADG has lots to offer.

What does the chapter offer in terms of learning?

The chapter offers a lot of growth in the learning sphere so one can explore different domains as mentioned above and choose the ones they think are the best for them and build projects on them!

I want to join ADG. Soâ€¦when do you recruit?

The tentative recruitment drive in at the start of every winter semester. Anything otherwise, shall be communicated through our social media platforms. Hence, keep checking them.

Do I need Apple devices such as a MacBook to be a part this chapter?

It's not at all necessary to have an apple device to be a part of ADG unless you need to be a part of iOS Domain, for iOS App Development Macbook is compulsory.

Hey, Do I need to be good in coding to be a part of this chapter?

If you want to be a part of the technical domains then yes, you need to have a decent technical knowledge when it comes to DSA (Don't get intimidated, practice what you know and you are good to go). Moreover, we have 3 more domains apart from technical which are Management, Design and Video Editing. So, if you are interested in either of those then you can definitely apply!

Is it okay if I'm not from any computer science related branches?

Yes, it's completely okay! All you need is enthusiasm to learn and you're good to go!

Cool! I think I am ready to join ADG. But where do I apply for recruitment?

The recruitment preliminary round will be a Quiz, conducted on the ADG-VIT website (adgvit.com), qualified candidates will be contacted by ADG for further round(s) based on their Quiz score.
